The Most Useful Twitter Bots

An open-source Twitter bot that lets you easily set reminders for public tweets. Mention "@RemindMe_OfThis" in the reply of any tweet and specify the time in natural English when you would like to reminded of that tweet. You could say things like in 2 days or in 12 hours or next week or even in 5 years. Check out the source on Github.
